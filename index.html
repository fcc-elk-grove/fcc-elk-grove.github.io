freeCodeCamp Elk Grove is a coding meetup based in Elk Grove, CA. We are a part of a larger group on Meetup called freeCodeCamp Greater Sacramento Area. We meet once a week and talk about various topics related to coding. Our meetups consist of workshops and discussions/co-working sessions.

We meet every Friday from 7pm-9pm.

Check out out page on Meetup for the latest info for our meetings.
