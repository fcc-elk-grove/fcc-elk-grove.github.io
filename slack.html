Slack Instructions

Thanks for joining the freeCodeCamp Elk Grove Slack group! Here are some things you can do to get started.

Get Started

First, post an introduction in the #introductions channel. Here are some things you can include:

  1. What’s your first name? (You only need to post this if your display name is different.)
  2. What do you currently do?
  3. How long have you been coding?
  4. What area(s) of programming are you focusing on (mobile, Android, React, front-end, back-end, etc.)?
Next, take a look at our main channel, #discussion. Feel free to post any coding questions or comments you have in there.

Optional

Share any projects you’re working on in #projects.

Post useful learning resources in #learning.

Share interesting job postings in #jobs.
